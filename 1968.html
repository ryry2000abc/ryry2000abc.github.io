1968

Mexico

Shortly before the 1968 Olympics, the Mexican Government (who hosted the olympics), violently repressed Mexican student protestors.hundreds of peaceful protesters were killed, and 1000s arrested. The protests were similar to US anti-vietnam and civil rights protests Two black runners did black power fist - a symbol in support of civil rights - during US national anthem. The atheletes were banned from competing, making the US civil rights movement an international issue. US-Soviet boxing match: George foreman v. Ionas Chepulis. Foreman (US) wins and waved a mini US flag to audience. The match represented another ideological showdown between the US and the USSR, as one more competitive manifestation of cold war tensions.
